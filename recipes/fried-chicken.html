Fried-Chicken

About Fried-Chicken

I used to buy Filipino fried chicken back home in Hawaii from manapua trucks.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon monosodium glutamate (such as Ajinomoto®) (Optional)
  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten
  • ½ bunch green onions, chopped
  • ¼ cup soy sauce (such as Aloha™ Shoyu)
  • 10 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 pounds chicken wings
  • vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Combine cornstarch, sugar, flour, salt, and monosodium glutamate in a large bowl. Add eggs, green onions, soy sauce, and garlic. Pat chicken dry and add to the sauce. Cover and refrigerate until marinated, 8 hours to overnight.
  2. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Fry chicken in the hot oil until golden brown, 15 to 25 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
