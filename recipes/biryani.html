Biryani

About Biryani

Biryani is a bit of a project and is time-consuming, but I've never been disappointed with the results. Since, it is such a festive dish, I usually make it for dinner parties. It's always a big hit.

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups basmati rice
  • ¼ cup cooking oil
  • 8 whole cloves
  • 4 black cardamom pods
  • 4 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 large onions, sliced thin
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1 pound lamb chops
  • salt to taste
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 green chile peppers, halved lengthwise
  • 2 teaspoons ground red pepper
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 7½ cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • ½ teaspoon saffron
  • 2 tablespoons warm milk
